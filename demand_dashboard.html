How to read this dashboard

  • Recent wk avg = last 4 weeks' average (Bangalore consumption); 4-wk Forecast = projected average per week May 4–31 2026.
  • Growth % = forecast avg vs trailing 4-week avg. ▲ = growth, ▼ = decline, ▬ = flat.
  • Festival/Event drivers are pulled from the calendar applied to each SKU. Click any card to see weekly history, forecast, and the full factor list.
  • Methodology: Forecast = Mar daily demand × extrapolated trend (Jan-Feb→Mar) × festival multiplier × seasonality adjustment (e.g., greens heat-yield, watermelon monsoon collapse).
